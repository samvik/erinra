About Erinra

Erinra is a secure password manager. The passwords are stored encrypted in your Google drive and no passwords ever leave your browser unencrypted. The only way to access the passwords is to know the master password for the database. In other words you only need to trust yourself.

This also means that if you forget your password no one can help you restore your passwords.
