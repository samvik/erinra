Access your passwords from anywhere without trusting anyone but yourself.

Secure

All data is stored encrypted in your google drive. No unencrypted passwords ever leavs your browser.

Erinra only needs access to its "appdata". It's not interested in who you are or what other files you may have stored on your drive.

Available

Erinra is available form all devices with a webbrowser, no need for specialized applications.

Simple

To host your own version of erinra you need a webserver capable of serving static files, which should be pretty much any webserver.
